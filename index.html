Activities

Popular Activities

Majestic open spaces, sun-soaked coasts, bucket-list activities and the finest hidden gems waiting for you to discover them. Our provinces have everything and more. So, where do you want to go today?

  • Adventure

    Parys Glamping Adventure

    Come enjoy a slow glamping adventure. River Raft on the mysterious Vaal River, Hike the ancient terrain all nestled in the Vredefort Dome World Heritage Site. A two billion year old site.

  • Cultural discoveries

    Bakone Malapa Open Air Museum

    Regarded as a living museum, the Bakone Malapa Northern Sotho Open-Air Museum near Polokwane is one of several museums and national monuments that bear testimony to South Africa's peoples

  • Coastal getaways

    Oyster Box

    Occupying a majestic location on the Umhlanga beachfront in KwaZulu-Natal province, The Oyster Box is famous for its remarkable coastal views

  • Bush retreats

    Dinokeng Safari @Lookout

    Safari Lodge is nestled within the malaria-free Dinokeng Game Reserve, near the town of Hammanskraal and just 52 km from Pretoria. This popular tourist destination is rich in traditional culture, history, and wildlife

  • Mountain escapes

    DRAKENSBERG MOUNTAIN RETREAT

    Experience tranquility at Drakensberg Mountain Retreat and adventure at Premier Resort Sani Pass with our exciting package.

  • Night Life escape

    EYADINI AND MAX LIFESTYLE

    Experience the Durban night in the most popular clubs with Great music, delicious food, a fountain of drinks, lots of selfies, friendly and trendy fun-lovers with smiles all around. No matter your type of fun you sure will have a night to remember
